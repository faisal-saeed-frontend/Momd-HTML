The road is closed - Dealing with roads blocks and detours

When traveling, the unexpected should be expected - Do not be discouraged. This life, be prepared and re-route.

If you are going to the store and the usual road is closed, what to you do? Turn around and go home? Give up and without? Re-route - ask for help, find another path to get where your are going. This is your journey.

“If at first you do not succeed try and try again.”

William Edward Hickson

Map of my dreams - Tips to success

  • Work with your TTS coach.
  • Care: Coordinate All Resources Effectively - get the most out of every program and service you are eligible for. (Your TTS Coach help you find the programs you want.)
  • Learn to make the most of the money you have. This is even more important when money is scarce.
  • Volunteer: Regardless of your age, race, or religion, People who volunteer do better physically, emotionaly and economically. Volunteer as a stepping stone to open doors to achieve your dreams. Helping others help to you too!
  • Ask for help - build your dream team. Find family, friends, neighbours, co-workers who have faced similar struggles - ask them for their support, advie and direction. Your dream team supports your transition.
