How Do I Get There?

“The Road to Success is Always Under Construction.”

Lily Tomlin

For each of your dreams begin to think about

“How do I get there?” and
"Who can help me and support my efforts?"

If you dream is a home, what do you have to do?

  • Save money for a down payment
  • Improve my credit score
  • Move to a different location
  • Ask my mom for help

If your dream is to be a professional basketball player what do you have to do?

  • Stay in school
  • Get good grades to be eligible for the team
  • Practice + Be drug free
  • Go to college
  • Eat healthy
  • Ask my gym teacher for help

If your dream is to learn to read what do you have to do?

  • Find a reading class
  • Do my homework
  • Take a bus to get there
Learn to: ASK FOR HELP
