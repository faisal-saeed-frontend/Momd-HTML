Where Am I Today?

“Do What You Can – With What You Have – Where You Are.”

Theodore Roosevelt

Ok, now, knowing what you want, let's figure out how to get there.

Just like a mechanic repairing a car, making your dreams come true requires the right tools. With the right tools and supports you are positioned for success. For example, if I don't have a car, or dependable buses, getting and keeping a job is likely impossible. Let’s understand the areas in your life that create challenges to your success and make a plan to deal with them.

The Life Area Survey on the next page does just that. In the example on the next page, the Dream is to be a teacher. The score 3 for food tells us this client usually can get by. The client identified that housing and income are priorities.
